Resources and Styles

Duration

10 minutes

Goals

The goal here is to make resources available across multiple pages by moving them to a resource dictionary in your app class.

Required assets

The provided Resources folder for this exercise contains a subfolder named Complete with a solution you can use to check your work.

This lab is a continuation of the previous one. If you did not complete the previous exercise, you can use the Completed solution from the previous exercise as starter code for this exercise.

Challenge

You can use the high-level description here to work through the lab on your own. Alternatively, you can use the more detailed instructions below if you would like step-by-step guidance.

  • Move your Styles from StandardTipPage.xaml to App.xaml.
  • Apply your Styles to a few elements in CustomTipPage.xaml.

Steps

Below are the step-by-step instructions to complete the exercise.

Page-level Resources

This section asks you to verify that resources defined on one page are not available in another page. Your app will not run correctly at the end of this section; however, you will fix it in the next section.

  1. Open the TipCalculator project.
  2. Open CustomTipPage.xaml.
  3. Set the infoLabelStyle resource as the Style of the billLabel as shown below. If you have time, you can apply the appropriate Style to the other labels on the page; however, it is not necessary. 
    4. <Label x:Name="billLabel" Text="Bill" Style="{StaticResource infoLabelStyle}" Grid.Row="0" Grid.Column="0" />
  4. Run the app on a platform of your choice. Navigate to CustomTipPage. The app should fail with a message saying the resource lookup failed. This is the correct behavior: the dictionary in StandardTipPage.xaml is not searched for resources used in CustomTipPage.xaml.

Application-level Resources

Here you will move your styles to an application-wide resource dictionary so they will be available throughout your entire app.

  1. Open App.xaml.
  2. Move your two Styles from the dictionary in StandardTipPage.xaml to App.xaml.
  3. Run the app on a platform of your choice. Navigate to CustomTipPage and verify the app now works correctly.

Summary

Here you defined Styles in an application-wide dictionary and verified that they are available from every page of your app.
